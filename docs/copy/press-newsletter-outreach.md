# Press & Newsletter Outreach — Traek v1.0 Launch

**Date:** 2026-03-08 (send outreach Week -1, before 2026-03-10 launch)
**Task:** [TRK-50](/issues/TRK-50)
**Goal:** 5-10 newsletter placements; 2-3 articles/features

## Outreach Strategy

- Contact newsletters **5-7 days before launch** (not day-of — too late)
- Offer exclusive early access to writers who want to try it before covering it
- Personalize each pitch to the newsletter's audience angle
- Follow up once, 2 days before launch, if no reply
