# Træk Meetup-in-a-Box

> A complete kit for community members who want to organize a local Træk or spatial AI UI meetup.
> Everything you need to host a successful event — from venue checklist to slide deck structure.

---

## Who This Is For

Community members who want to:
- Run a local developer meetup featuring Træk
- Present Træk at an existing JavaScript/AI/frontend group
- Host a "build with Træk" workshop at a hackathon or coworking space

No prior experience organizing events required. This kit covers everything.

---

## What Træk Will Provide to Organizers

Email `community@gettraek.com` with your event details. We will provide:

- [ ] Official Træk swag (stickers, die-cut postcards) shipped to your venue
- [ ] Digital event graphics (banner, social card, logo files)
- [ ] Speaker prep call (30 min with a Træk team member)
- [ ] A co-promotion tweet/post from the @traek account
- [ ] Post-event write-up featured on the Træk blog (for events with 20+ attendees)
- [ ] Optional: virtual guest speaker from the Træk team (subject to availability)

## Event Formats

### Format A: Demo Night (60 min, no prep required)

Best for established meetup groups where Træk is one of several talks.

```
00:00  Intro (5 min) — what is Træk, why spatial AI UIs
00:05  Live demo of Træk Playground (10 min)
00:15  Code walkthrough: spinning up @traek/svelte or @traek/react (20 min)
00:35  Q&A (15 min)
00:50  Show & tell: attendees who built with Træk (10 min)
01:00  End
```

**What you need:** Laptop with Træk demo running, projector/screen, attendees.

---

### Format B: Workshop (2–3 hours, light prep)

Best for a dedicated Træk workshop or hackathon intro session.

```
Part 1 — Why (30 min)
  - The problem with linear chat (5 min)
  - Spatial canvas concepts (10 min)
  - Live demo: playground tour (15 min)

Part 2 — Quick Start (45 min)
  - Install @traek/svelte or @traek/react (5 min)
  - Walk through starter template (20 min)
  - Connect a real AI provider (OpenAI / Anthropic) (20 min)

Break (15 min)

Part 3 — Build (60–90 min)
  - Attendees build their own Træk-powered app
  - Organizer circulates to help
  - End with 5-min demos from 2–3 attendees
```

**What you need:** All attendees with laptops, Node.js/npm installed, API key for AI provider (tell attendees in advance), stable Wi-Fi.

---

### Format C: Presentation Only (20–30 min)

Best as a lightning talk at a general JavaScript or AI meetup.

Choose one of the three prepared talks from `docs/community/conference-talks.md`:
- **Beyond Linear Chat** — broad audience, problem-first
- **Building Spatial AI UIs** — technical audience, code-forward
- **Tree-Structured Thinking** — design/ideas audience, no code

## Venue Checklist

### Physical Requirements

- [ ] Projector or large monitor (1080p minimum) with HDMI/USB-C adapter
- [ ] Microphone (for groups larger than 25)
- [ ] Stable Wi-Fi (test before event — streaming AI responses need internet)
- [ ] Enough seats + standing room buffer (plan for 20% more than RSVPs)
- [ ] Power strips for workshop format

### Location Ideas

- Coworking spaces (often free for community events)
- Company offices (tech companies frequently host meetups)
- Libraries with event rooms
- University maker spaces or CS department labs
- Hacker/maker spaces

## During the Event

### Opening (5 min)

Welcome, logistics (Wi-Fi, bathrooms, recording notice), brief intro of Træk, housekeeping.

**Suggested opening line:**
> "Quick show of hands — how many of you have had more than 10 AI chat tabs open at once because you needed to explore multiple directions at the same time?"

[pause for hands]

> "That's the problem we're here to talk about."

### Managing Q&A

- Collect questions in a shared doc or Slido rather than hand-raising (more inclusive)
- If a question goes deep and off-topic, offer to follow up after the session
- Reserve "how do I contribute?" questions for end — bridge to Discord invite

### Closing

- Share Discord invite QR code + link
- Mention next event / follow the @traek account
- Take a group photo if attendees consent — tag @traek on social

## FAQ for Organizers

**Do I need permission from Træk to run an event?**
No. Anyone can run a Træk community event. Email us if you want swag and co-promotion support, but it's not required.

**Can I charge for admission?**
Community meetups should be free to attend. If you're running a paid workshop or conference session featuring Træk, let us know — we want to support good events and may have suggestions.

**What if I'm not an expert in Træk?**
That's fine. The Format A demo night requires only that you can run the playground and explain what it does. You don't need to be a contributor. Learn alongside your attendees.

**Can I translate this kit?**
Yes. We'd love localized versions. Email us if you'd like help or want to share your translation.

**What if fewer people show up than expected?**
Small is fine. Even 5 engaged developers exploring Træk together is a success. Adjust the format to be more conversational, less presentation-focused.
